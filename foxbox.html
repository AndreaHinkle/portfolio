Foxbox

Project: Foxbox - An evernote clone created as a student project

View Invision mockup | View Github prototype

Team members:

  • Designer (myself), and my UX mentor
  • My role: Design and coding, with help from course materials
Situation:
  • Creatives and developers often end up having their useful documents and reference materials scattered across a myriad of services.
  • It can be difficult to find what you need at the right time.
Action:
  • I created a survey to find how people viewed and saved information they found online, then created user stories and personas based on the answers. From that I decided on the features needed to make Foxbox a minimally viable product and began mapping out user flows. Then the wireframing process began.
UX Deliverables:
  • Personas
  • User research
  • Wireframes - tested and reiterated
Results:
  • A basic responsive demo site
