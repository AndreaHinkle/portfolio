Nevermore

Project: Nevermore - an online graphic t shirt retailer created as a student project

View Invision mockup

Click to enlarge

Team members:

  • Designer (myself), and my UX mentor
  • My role: Design
Situation:
  • Users should have access to a high quality, responsive shopping experience.
  • I determined that providing a simplified version of the Threadless experience would be a boon, as they’re known for high quality but have grown into so many different product lines that navigating threadless.com can be confusing.
Action:
  • I created a competitive analysis of 3 other top competitors in the space, to determine a way that this shop could set itself apart. I also wanted to make sure that the newsletter sign up was prominent, as it’s easier to sell to people who are already familiar with you than it is to attract new site visitors.
UX/UI Deliverables:
  • Competitive analysis
  • Wireframes
  • Sample product designs
Results:
  • HD wireframes and invision prototype
